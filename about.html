About Fluxa

Built in Melbourne. Made for learners everywhere.

Our story

Fluxa was born out of frustration. After experiencing the same complaints millions of language learners share — energy gates that cut off learning mid-flow, grammar taught through random, meaningless sentences, and subscriptions that feel more like traps than services — we decided to build something better.

Something that actually respects your time and intelligence. Something that teaches language the way a good teacher would: by explaining the rule, showing you an example, then letting you practise without artificial barriers.

Fluxa is built by DLV Digital Solutions Pty Ltd, a Melbourne-based software company passionate about making language learning accessible, effective, and genuinely enjoyable. We use cutting-edge AI technology and thoughtful design to give every learner a first-class experience — for free.

What we stand for

🎓

Learner First

Every decision we make starts with one question: does this help the learner? Features that serve business goals at the expense of the learner experience don't make it in.

💰

Transparent Pricing

We believe in honest, simple pricing. Spanish and French are free forever. Every other language is a single $4.99 payment. No subscriptions. No surprise charges. Ever.

📚

Grammar Matters

You can't truly learn a language by memorising phrases. Understanding the rules — why the sentence works, not just what it is — is the foundation of real fluency.
