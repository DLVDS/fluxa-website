Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

DLV Digital Solutions Pty Ltd ("we", "our", "us") operates the Fluxa language learning application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use our App.

ABN: 71 697 056 590. Registered in Victoria, Australia.

By downloading and using Fluxa, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

2. What Data We Collect

Fluxa is designed with a privacy-first approach. We collect minimal data:

Data stored locally on your device

  • Your learning progress, XP points, lesson history, and streak count
  • Your selected learning language and CEFR level
  • In-app purchase records (language pack purchases)
  • Notification preferences and settings
  • Your mistakes review deck and seen cultural tips

Data sent to third-party services

  • AI Conversation (Anthropic API): When you use the AI conversation feature, your typed messages are sent to Anthropic's API to generate responses. We do not store these conversations on our servers. See Anthropic's privacy policy at anthropic.com for details on how they handle data.
  • Analytics: We may collect anonymised, aggregated usage statistics (such as feature usage frequency) to improve the App. This data cannot be used to identify individual users.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the Fluxa language learning service
  • To track your progress and maintain your streak
  • To process and restore in-app purchases
  • To deliver Word of the Day push notifications (only if you enable this feature)
  • To improve lessons, fix bugs, and enhance the App experience

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising. We do not share your data with third parties except as described in this policy.

4. Third-Party Services

Anthropic API (AI Conversations)

The AI conversation feature ("Sofia") is powered by Anthropic's Claude API. Conversation messages are transmitted to Anthropic to generate responses. No personal identifying information (name, email, location) is attached to these requests. Conversations are not stored on our servers.

Apple App Store

In-app purchases on iOS are processed entirely by Apple. We receive a confirmation of purchase from Apple but never see or store your payment details. Apple's privacy policy governs how they handle your payment data.

Google Play

In-app purchases on Android are processed entirely by Google. We receive a confirmation of purchase from Google but never see or store your payment details. Google's privacy policy governs how they handle your payment data.

5. Data Storage

All personal learning data (progress, history, settings) is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data does not leave your device and is not transmitted to our servers. When you uninstall the App, this data is permanently deleted from your device.

6. In-App Purchases

Language pack purchases ($4.99 per language) are processed entirely by Apple App Store or Google Play. We never see, collect, or store your credit card or payment details. Purchase receipts are validated through Apple and Google's respective secure systems. All purchases are one-time; we do not operate subscription billing.

7. Push Notifications

Fluxa may request permission to send push notifications for the Word of the Day feature. This permission is entirely optional. If you grant permission, daily notifications are scheduled locally on your device. You can disable notifications at any time through the Fluxa Settings screen or your device's notification settings. We do not use notifications for marketing without your explicit consent.

8. Children's Privacy

Fluxa is suitable for users aged 13 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@fluxa.com.au and we will delete it promptly.

9. Data Retention

Learning data is stored locally on your device and retained as long as you have the App installed. Uninstalling the App permanently deletes all locally stored data. We do not retain data on our servers beyond what is necessary for purchase verification and anonymous analytics.

10. Your Rights

Under Australian Privacy Act 1988 and applicable law, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@fluxa.com.au.

11. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect any information processed through our services. The AI conversation feature uses HTTPS encryption in transit. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
